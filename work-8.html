Water tower challenge

Optimizing a Biodegradable
Water Tower with FEA

Project Overview

As part of the FEA Water Tower Challenge 2025, our team designed and analyzed a lightweight, biodegradable water tower, balancing structural efficiency with environmental responsibility.

Using ANSYS, we conducted extensive static and buckling analyses, refining our design to ensure optimal load-bearing capacity and resistance to structural instability. By iterating through simulations, we identified key stress points and optimized material distribution, enhancing both performance and sustainability.

Our accurate FEA predictions were validated when constructing the water tower using pasta, as our design successfully supported the highest weight in the competition. By closely matching theoretical analysis with real-world performance, we secured 1st place, demonstrating the precision of our structural simulations and the strength of our design.
